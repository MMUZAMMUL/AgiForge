Questions

Is it really free?

Yes. The core app and 50+ specialists are free forever. You connect your own free Groq API key (no credit card) and you're running. Premium specialist packs are an optional one-time purchase.

Do I need to install anything?

No. AgentForge runs entirely in your browser. On mobile you can tap "Add to Home Screen" to use it like a native app, with offline support.

Where does my data go?

Nowhere we control. There's no backend — your API key, chats, and memory live in your browser's local storage. The only network calls are to your AI provider and optional search/code-execution services.

What can I actually build with it?

Product launches, marketing campaigns, code and architecture, security reviews, GIS analysis, financial models, game design docs — anything the 247 specialists cover, often by chaining several together in a pipeline.

Is the All-Access purchase a subscription?

No. It's a one-time payment that unlocks every specialist plus lifetime updates.
